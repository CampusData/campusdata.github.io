About the organization

CampusData.org makes data on college campus easily accessible and usable. We are a group of students from Columbia, Penn, Rutgers, CMU, Duke and Waterloo, and we are working hard to create a culture of open data at our universities. We want you to empower students at your school to do the same, and help you achieve this goal.

Want to learn more?

We have put together a guidebook, that describes the work that we're doing at our universities to create open campus data. It is a useful resource for anyone that's looking to read the stories of the campus data revolution, and learn from them to bring open campus data to their own schools.
